About Us

The School Academy is a lighthouse of hope

The School Academy is a lighthouse of hope, driven by a mission to uplift lives and build brighter futures. Inspired by the teachings of Sai and guided by the visionary leadership of our founder and president, Mrs Anita Shriwas, we are committed to transforming communities through education, empowerment, and well-being.
